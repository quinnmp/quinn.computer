Research

Since October 2023, I have been doing research with the Personal Robotics Lab and the WEIRD Lab in association with the Paul G. Allen School at UW. I have had the priviledge of working with Professors Siddhartha Srinivasa and Abhishek Gupta.
My research has covered many domains of robotics - my first major project was a complete Inverse Kinematics Solver overhaul for our chopsticks robot, Tycho. I implemented a novel method of using simulator end effector control to solve IK in a simulated environment. Additionally, I worked on a paper on Continuity-Based Corrective Labels in the context of Robot Behavior Cloning, awaiting acceptance into IROS 2024 (this site will be updated with the link).
